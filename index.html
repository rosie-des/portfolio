About Me

🌸 Photo placeholder

Research-led. Accessibility-driven. Always curious.

Hi, I'm Madison (Rosie) Stewart — a UX designer and researcher based in Atlanta, GA, currently pursuing my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction.

My design philosophy starts with empathy and ends with evidence. I believe the best products are built on real user insights, designed with accessibility as a constraint (not an afterthought), and tested until they actually work for the people they serve.

I'm especially drawn to the intersection of AI, emerging technology, and human behavior — how we can use new tools to design experiences that feel genuinely personal and humane. I also love design engineering: the place where UX thinking meets code.

Outside of design: I read obsessively, shoot film photography, and graphic design for fun.
